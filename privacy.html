Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Steaux Clippings ("the app") is an automated content clipping bot. This policy explains how we handle information.

Data We Collect

The app accesses your TikTok account solely to post approved video clips on your behalf. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data beyond what is required to authenticate with TikTok's API.

How We Use Your Data

Your TikTok access token is used exclusively to publish video content to your account. It is stored locally on the server running the bot and is never shared with third parties.
